#### Key Qualitative Feedback (English)

**Search & Navigation:**
- "I just use Google to find things... let the AI tell me what I would be hoping to find" (Mohamed)
- "PSAB page, it's not easy to navigate to current work — you have to go to the standard section instead" (Amanda)
- "Check upcoming PSAS Project Listing — very difficult to find, even though I access it often"
- "Clear status for each standard-setting project (current project pages have too much information)"
- "Non-standards projects like Strategic Plan consultation are difficult to find"

**Content Issues:**
- "Webpages often have errors, inaccuracies, or are unable to be located"
- "The site web n'est pas a jour, l'information tarde a etre mise a jour"
- "Uploading a response to document for comment seems very unstable — no confirmation email"
- "Meeting summaries are not posted based on date posted, but when they occurred" (Melissa)
- "Links often do not work"

**Missing Content:**
- Past project documents (comments, archives)
- Timely meeting decision summaries
- Resources for adoption of new standards
- Older documents for historical context
- CSSB standards hard to locate

**Feature Requests:**
- Volunteer spotlights/profiles
- Plain-language tutorials for reading standards
- Summary of non-authoritative guidance by topic
- Better confirmation for form submissions
- Prior webinars and reference materials

### 2.2 French Survey (8 respondents)

#### Key French Feedback
- "Le site web n'est pas a jour, l'information tarde a etre mise a jour" (site not up to date)
- PDF language switching is painful — "il faut refaire tout le chemin sur la version du site dans l'autre langue"
- Requesting archives (e.g., PSAB annual plans from prior years)
- "Manque d'alignement avec les instances internationales"
- "Baux emphyteotiques" (emphyteutic leases — niche accounting topic request)

#### Notable French Respondent
A translator uses the site to find official terminology across EN/FR versions — the bilingual PDF switching UX is a real pain point for professional translators.

## 3. Cross-Reference with Our PRD

### Architecture Alignment
| FRAS Architecture | Our PRD | Status |
|------------------|---------|--------|
| 9 standards sections | 11 in our discovery | **Discrepancy** — xlsx has 9, our discovery found 11. Likely the xlsx predates IRCSS and one other addition. |
| Board committees | Not in Phase 1/2 PRD | **Gap** — committees are a significant content area (11+ per board). Currently not in our collections. |
| AASB "Initiatives" section | Not in PRD | **Gap** — Audits of LCEs, Sustainability Assurance, Technology are AASB-specific initiatives, not standard projects. |
| My Account (3 sections) | Phase 2 Epic 17 auth | **Aligned** — My Subscription, My Profile, Submit Comment Form |
| Effective Dates pages | Not explicitly in PRD | **Consider** — each standards section has Effective Dates. May need a dedicated view or filter. |
| Recruitment Guidelines (RASOC) | Not in PRD | **Minor gap** — RASOC-specific content page |
